Synopsis

This project is a history quiz webpage with a wide variety of topics. It allows players to create profiles and choose quiz topics to start or continue. It includes both individual or group challenge quiz formats. The group challenges are created by a group creator; who names the group, selects the quiz the group will take, and invites players to accept the challenge. Individual quiz takers can take any quiz as many times as they like and their progress will be saved and reported for the most recent attempt of each quiz.
